group of 6 friends into 3 groups each with size {3, 2, 1}friends.

 Pick 3 for 1st group C(6, 3) => remains 3
◼ Pick 2 for 2nd group C(3, 2) => remains 1
◼ Pick 1 for 3rd group C(1, 1) => remains 0
◼ C(6, 3) * C(3, 2) * C(2, 1) = 6! / (3!*2!*1!)
===================================

our group of 18 friends into 5 groups each with size {5, 5, 5, 2, 1}friends

◼ If we take order into account, then 18!/(5!*5!*5!*2!*1!)
